Refuge Counseling Co

 

 

 I am a therapist who loves helping people grow and work through difficult emotions and experiences. My passion is working with adults, adolescents, and parents on improving relationships, working through life-transitions, family problems (past and present), and other emotional struggles encountered. Anxiety and depression can feel debilitating, but it doesn't have to be. I have seen the deep emotional struggle many young people face, that can also impact adulthood. I have also had the opportunity to work with parents on improving their relationship with their teens and children, while setting consistent boundaries to support them.
 
Therapy should be a space for healing and to process, free from judgements, so my aim is to create a space for my clients where they know they can be their true self. My therapeutic approach is grounded in evidence-based treatment, and adapted to the unique struggles you are facing.
 
Through my own personal journey in psychotherapy, I have been able to feel first-hand the impact and healing that can occur with the right therapist. My desire is to meet others where they are, and offer a safe, inviting space where clients are able to explore, grow and heal. I know how difficult it is to take this first step, but know you are not alone!
