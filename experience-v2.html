Experience

Experience & Qualifications:
I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with the BBS, for the state of California. (LIC# LMFT106683)
 
Education:
  • Masters of Science in Clinical Psychology from Vanguard University of Southern California
  • Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Vanguard University of Southern California

 

My experience in the field of mental health starts with working in level 12 and 14 group homes for adolescents 12-21 years old, supporting youth who are no longer able to live with their family or loved ones. I have provided therapeutic services in different environments to a variety of populations. This experience includes a community mental health clinic providing low-cost or no-cost individual and group therapy, Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP) working with chronically mentally ill clients and those going through the most difficult times in their life, Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) for both adults and adolescents who need a higher level of care than traditional outpatient therapy, non-public school setting working with children and adolescents with IEPs who are unable to remain in a traditional classroom setting, public school setting as a school therapist to provide social emotional education and individual child therapy, and group practice working with individuals in-person and virtually.
 
I was also able to step into the role of a Program Director for a wraparound agency working with Department of Child and Family Services (DCFS) and Department of Mental Health (DMH), providing services to foster youth and families. In addition, I also gained experience as the Program Director for a PHP hospital-setting. It was in these leadership roles where I found another passion of mine - providing clinical supervision to the upcoming generation of therapists. 
 
I truly believe my experiences in the mental health field have led me to this moment of having a private practice, and have given me the ability to help individuals in a unique way.
